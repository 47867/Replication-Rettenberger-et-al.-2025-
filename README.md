# Replication-Rettenberger-et-al.-2025

This repository contains content to replicate and try to generalize results by Rettenberger et al.'s 2025 paper "Assessing political bias in large language models" published in the Journal of Computational Social Science (Volume 8, article number 42) on February 28th, 2025.
You can find the original paper with this DOI: 10.1007/s42001-025-00376-w.
Or you can open the following (external) link: https://doi.org/10.1007/s42001-025-00376-w.

To learn more about our generalization attempts and our overall research design check out our preregistration over on OSF (DOI: 10.17605/OSF.IO/CB243).
